# Material Samples

Use this protocol when finishes may shift under real site conditions.

## Pattern

1. Build a sample ladder: baseline, stretch, and fallback.
2. Review samples in showroom light and on site.
3. Check morning daylight, workday light, and evening artificial light when relevant.
4. Record the chosen sample, rejected samples, and the visible reason.
5. Remove or generalize supplier, price, address, and private photo details before promotion.

## Cross-Project Evidence

- Project Atrium: acoustic fabric looked darker under reception lighting than in the showroom.
- Project Garden Flat: cabinet fronts showed fingerprints differently near cooking and living zones.
- Project Lantern Row: corridor floor samples shifted warmer under low-glare fixtures.

## Decision Template

```text
Sample set:
Project type:
Lighting checked:
Chosen option:
Rejected option:
Visible reason:
Privacy action:
```

## Privacy Actions

- Supplier name: generalize to material category.
- Exact quote: generalize to entry, mid-range, or premium.
- Site address: generalize to project type.
- Private photo: block and describe only the material or lighting behavior.
- Access detail: block completely as `[BLOCKED: site access detail]`.
