# Global Studio Wiki

This is the client-neutral global vault for reusable architecture and interior-design lessons. Entries here must be safe to reuse across projects and must not expose client identity, addresses, access details, private photos, exact budgets, or supplier quotes.

## Promotion Rules

- Promote a lesson only after it applies across at least two projects.
- Strip names, addresses, private schedules, photos, invoices, quotes, and access details.
- Keep the design principle, the conditions where it applies, and the safe counterexample.
- Link back to the local queue only when the local source is also privacy-safe.

## Current Entries

- `material-samples.md`: sample ladder and site-light review protocol.

## Reusable Patterns

- Replace personal routines with design-relevant traffic patterns.
- Replace exact prices with broad bands: entry, mid-range, premium.
- Replace private photos with short descriptions of the observable design constraint.
- Block access details completely, even when they explain scheduling friction.
